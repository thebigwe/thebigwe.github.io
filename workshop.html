The workshop analyses and maps emerging social networks as an inspiration and as a source of strength for the Big We in Europe. The guests of this workshop develop a cartography of relations among the more than 100 representatives of initiatives that are present at Slow Politics : How do they relate to each other, to national politics and to other forces in Europe? In the course of this, a visualization of relationships will be produced in the form of a dynamic map of Europe.
